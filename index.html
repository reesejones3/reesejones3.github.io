About Us

Glacée provides a plethora of drink options, with the twist of combining alcohol and ice cream. The menu consists of high quality alcohol from all over the world, ice cream with dairy free options and a variety of toppings. We aim to please our customers with executing any and every combination the customer requests.

Bartenders

Glacée employs passionate bartenders aiming to please every customer based each of their taste palates. The bartenders have all come with at least 3 years of experience and have mastered the art of making exquisite beverages. Each one has experienced the professional training from the bartender that started with Glacée years ago.
